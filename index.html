Every major decision depends on documents. Validity makes the logic inside them visible, testable, and verifiable.

AI is now generating documents that govern capital, liability, and obligation faster than humans can review them, without verifying whether the commitments inside them hold together. A contradiction that goes undetected is capital committed on a false premise. Validity is the verification layer.

Validity extracts every binding commitment in your document, translates it into formal logic, and runs it through a theorem prover. In seconds, you know whether the document is internally consistent — or whether it contains a provable contradiction.
