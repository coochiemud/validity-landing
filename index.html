What It Detects

Validity identifies internal inconsistencies in structured investment documents, including:

  • Valuation assumptions inconsistent with stated methodology
  • Financial projections incompatible with operational constraints
  • Risk disclosures misaligned with performance claims
  • Covenant definitions inconsistent with facility terms
  • Assumption dependency chains not structurally reconciled
  • Capitalisation tables and financial models that do not reconcile

Deterministic. Reproducible. Auditable.
