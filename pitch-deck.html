Bad Reasoning Costs Billions

High-stakes decisions fail not from missing data, but from:

  • Assumptions treated as facts
  • Causal leaps without mechanism
  • Risks buried in confident language

Review processes reward fluency, not truth.

The Solution

Validity audits the logical structure of high-stakes documents—exposing hidden reasoning flaws before they become costly decisions.

How it works:

  • Upload investment memo, policy brief, legal argument, or strategy document
  • AI analyzes reasoning structure: claims, premises, logical chain, hidden assumptions
  • Receive diagnostic report: reasoning score, flagged failures, actionable fixes

Built for: Investment teams, policy units, legal groups, strategy leaders
